The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Dr. Muhammad Yunus, the Nobel Peace Prize winner and pioneer of microfinance, envisions a Bangladesh where poverty is eradicated through inclusive economic growth and social innovation. In his ideal future, every citizen has access to opportunities that empower them to become self-reliant and contribute to a thriving society. Dr. Yunus advocates for a shift towards social business models, where profits are reinvested into the community to address critical issues such as healthcare, education, and environmental sustainability. He believes that harnessing the entrepreneurial spirit of the Bangladeshi people, especially women and marginalized groups, will lead to a more equitable and prosperous nation. By fostering innovation, collaboration, and a commitment to social justice, Dr. Yunus envisions a Bangladesh that is not only economically strong but also a model for sustainable development worldwide."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
