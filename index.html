Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladeshi entrepreneurs are increasingly leveraging e-commerce platforms to create innovative business models, connecting local artisans with global markets.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is prioritizing sustainability by integrating eco-friendly practices in agriculture, renewable energy, and urban development to combat climate change and promote resource conservation.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh is rapidly improving its infrastructure through major investments in roads, bridges, and urban development, aiming to support economic growth and connectivity

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh is transforming lives, fostering gender equality, and enabling communities to drive their own development.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Dr. Muhammad Yunus, the Nobel laureate and founder of the Grameen Bank, has often inspired the world with his thoughts on social business and empowerment. He famously stated, "Poverty is not created by the poor. Poverty is created by the systems we have put in place." This highlights his belief that innovative solutions can be developed to eradicate poverty by empowering individuals, particularly women, to become entrepreneurs. Yunus emphasizes that "the best way to help the poor is to give them the opportunity to help themselves." His vision that “businesses should be a vehicle for social change” underlines the principle that profit can coexist with social responsibility. By advocating for microfinance and social entrepreneurship, Yunus has taught us that the power of ideas can foster dignity and create pathways to a better life for millions".
